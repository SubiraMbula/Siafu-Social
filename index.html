Youth are a vast source of innovation, ideas, and solutions

Our ecosystem is powered by young men and women who have local knowledge of where they live and are looking for employment and learning opportunities in the retail marketplace.

The retail universe in Africa is at best chaotic, unstructured, fragmented and extremely difficult to navigate. We have realised an urgent need for alternative general trade engagement models that reduces its unpredictability and offers brands an opportunity to survive in the market.


Our value proposition
  • We are powering retail optimization through great relationships powered by local knowledge

  • We provide cost effective, efficient and result based outsourced solution to brand owners to ensure that their products and service reach consumers

  • We provide employment opportunities and impart entrepreneurial skills to the youths

We are alive to the challenges of last mile product and service availability

  • 01 Marketing Monologues

    Mass market advertising lead to cut throat practices that result in no RRP adherence, new entrants with very little chance of survival & the customer without a voice/say.

  • 02 Fragmented & Unstructured retail

    The population is inundated with messages that are irrelevant and meaningless to their context leading to disengagement.

  • 03 Disjointed Relationships

    Lack of consistent friendly faces and the place of personal relationships between brand owners and retailers. Emergence of technology that has cut off any human touch to brands owners and last mile interface

  • 04 Expensive Solutions

    The solutions available to businesses are targeted to large and multinational enterprises and not customized for the local context. Last mile delivery becomes a cost that erodes the bottomline.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIAFU SOCIAL?

SIAFU SOCIAL is an ecosystem powered by technology that connects unemployed youth and last mile distribution opportunities that enable them to earn decent income.

How do I use SIAFU SOCIAL?

To use SIAFU SOCIAL, simply go to the Siafu Social website and apply. You will then receive an email directing you to an application form that you will be required to complete. If successful you will be called for a physical interview and if you qualify you will receive a download link, download the SIAFU SOCIAL app on your smartphone, create an account, and begin to earn by completing tasks.The signup is ABSOLUTELY FREE.

How do I get a task?

To get a task, open the SIAFU SOCIAL app and enter your credentials. SIAFU SOCIAL will then match you with a nearby tasks in your area

How do I get paid for my completed tasks?

SIAFU SOCIAL will credit your account through the app, you will be able to see your available and actual balances. You can then proceed to withdraw amounts from your actual balance menu to your Mpesa or account.You will choose weekly or bi-weekly withdrwal option suitable for you.

What happens if I move to another area?

When you move from the currently area of residence, You will be required to make an application through the App. Upon successful completion of the process your new location will reflect on your account together with your work history.

Is SIAFU SOCIAL safe?

SIAFU SOCIAL takes safety very seriously and has implemented a number of safety features, live location checks, real-time GPS tracking, and the ability to share your location with friends, family or other Siafus.

How much does SIAFU SOCIAL pay?

Payment on SIAFU SOCIAL depends on a number of tasks you have been allocated, payment amount depends on each specific task and is visible before you embark on the task

Can I use SIAFU SOCIAL in other countries?

Currently we're only active in Kenya.Sign up to our newsletter so that we let you know when we enter into to a new country..

How do I contact SIAFU SOCIAL customer support?

You can contact SIAFU SOCIAL customer support through the app, contact us section,sending us message through contact us form on this website,our social media handles,WhatsApp message to our support number on this website or by sending an email to hello@siafusocial.org.
